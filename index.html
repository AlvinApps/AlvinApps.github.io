Privacy Policy for ColorJan

Effective Date: July 21, 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to the ColorJan mobile application (the “Application”). ColorJan is a simple game featuring a collection of mini-games. The Application does not require you to enter any personal information; it operates locally on your device and displays advertisements via Google AdMob.

1. Information Collection and Use

The Application itself does not collect any data you manually enter (e.g., name, email). However, the Application and its integrated Google AdMob SDK may automatically collect or infer the following:

  • Network & Device Data: IP address (for approximate location), operating system version, device model.
  • App Usage Details: Screens visited, date and time of each visit, time spent on each screen, and total session duration.
  • Device Identifiers: Advertising ID.
  • Diagnostic & Performance Data: Crash logs, performance metrics.
  • Cookies & Similar Technologies: Used by AdMob to deliver and measure ads.

No precise location (GPS) or other personally identifiable information (PII) is collected by the Application itself.

2. Communication and Marketing

We may use contact information provided through any means to send you important updates, notices, and promotional materials about the Application. You can opt out of marketing communications at any time by contacting us at the email below.

3. Purpose of Data Use

  • Advertising: To serve and personalize ads through Google AdMob based on anonymized data.
  • Analytics & Improvements: Aggregate usage data to improve gameplay, fix bugs, and optimize ad delivery.

4. Third-Party Services & Access

  • When we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others, to investigate fraud, or to respond to a government request.
  • With our trusted service providers who work on our behalf, provided they adhere to this Privacy Policy and do not use the information for other purposes.

6. Opt-Out & Controls

  • Advertising ID Reset/Disable: Reset or opt out of interest-based ads via device settings: Settings > Google > Ads > Reset advertising ID or Opt out of Ads Personalization.
  • Uninstall: To stop all data collection, uninstall the Application via your device’s standard process.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

All data collected by AdMob is retained by Google per their policies. The Application stores no PII. To request deletion of any data provided or collected, follow Google’s instructions for resetting or deleting your Advertising ID, or contact us; we will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

8. Children & Families Policy

ColorJan is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. Ads served to child-directed audiences are limited to general-audience (G) content if configured in AdMob.
